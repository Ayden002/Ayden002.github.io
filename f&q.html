FAQ

Q: How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

A: At http://print.hz.nl can you log in with your HZ account and upload a file that you want to print, using your HZ pas by any HZ printers you will be able print them.

Q: How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?

A: At a HZ printer you can scan your pas and choose scan and the files will be send to your mail box.

Q: What do you need to do when you are sick ?

A: Contact the teachers/helpdesk at school and stay home. Follow the lessons on stream to compensate for that unusual sickness.

Q: How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

A: On the home page on Self Service Portal you can choose Reservations & visitors and you will be able to book a room.

Q: What if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

A: active your HZ pas at first floor and when you want to parking there you can scan your HZ pas.

Q: How to get a Student travel product?

Q: On DUO you can find all the information you need and the requirements for it.
