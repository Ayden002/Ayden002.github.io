My motivation

Motivation/Study Choice

At first, a large part of the reason I chose to study this was that I yearned for the IT industry when I was a child. I felt that typing code made me feel like a hacker, and as I grew older, I gradually realized that IT nowadays is everywhere. Computer programming languages are used in many places. Learning here is more about practice and operation, without a lot of theoretical knowledge and written examinations.

I was injured in a game two years ago. At that time, I felt that my life was coming to an end. I couldn't do what I loved anymore. I kept myself in the room for two months without studying or socializing. I vent my emotions by overeating and playing computer games, but my teacher has always encouraged me to get out of the shadows and asked me to study in a country I always wanted to go to. In the end, I chose HZ University of Applied Sciences and took HBO-ICT courses. I know that studying this major will be very difficult, especially for students who have no experience, but this has also become my motivation. I have to prove to myself that as long as I persist, I can do a lot of things.

Personal SWOT Analysis

I am best at focusing on one thing. When I am sure what I want to accomplish, I can turn all my attention to what I need to do without being interfered by external factors, and I am very willing to help others. At the same time, I am a person who is willing to accept criticism from others. I think these suggestions can make me better.My weakness is that I sometimes feel very hesitant, and I can’t determine by myself whether it’s the right thing to do. For example, when I was working on my homework and encountered an answer that I was not sure whether it was correct or not, my first reaction was not to determine it by myself but to ask other people, which greatly delayed my study time.

Acknowledge and accept my weaknesses. If you are busy denying the existence of weaknesses, you cannot turn weaknesses into strengths.Carelessness is a big problem for me. Being careless in my previous exams will cost me a large part of my scores. This problem will also affect my future career, especially in programming languages.In my free time, I also try to find a part-time job, but I prefer to find a job related to my major after studying the HBO-ICT course.
